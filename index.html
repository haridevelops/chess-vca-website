Vadapalani Chess Academy (VCA) was founded by K.Pandian F/O P.Karthikeyan (International Chess Grand Master) and P.Ganesh (Former Tamil Nadu State Chess Champion).

VCA Offers a Proper and Professional Chess Coaching for Children from the Scratch to the Advanced level will be trained by the Chess professionals Since we are in Chess field for more than 20 years. VCA make sure to give best experience for each individual kid. We help children to learn Chess in a traditional way though Chess is an art which also helps their inner transformation. We have Initiated VCA with a Vision to create more beautiful minds and young talents, also to spread the soul of chess by expanding it all over the field. VCA is a licensed Affiliated to Chennai District Chess Association.
